用鏡頭記錄世界，用照片講述故事

Capture the world with your lens, tell stories with your photos.

我是陳鼎文，一名熱愛旅遊的攝影師， 多年來，我的足跡遍布全日本，從繁華都市到遙遠的鄉村，每一個地方都給了我無盡的靈感和挑戰。我喜歡與當地人交流，了解他們的生活方式和故事，這使得我的作品更具有深度和情感。我始終相信，旅行攝影不僅僅是捕捉美景，更是記錄和分享不同文化的珍貴體驗。

I am Chen Dingwun, a passionate travel photographer. Over the years, my journeys have taken me all across Japan, from bustling cities to remote villages. Every place has provided me with endless inspiration and challenges. I enjoy interacting with locals to understand their lifestyles and stories, which adds depth and emotion to my work. I firmly believe that travel photography is not just about capturing beautiful scenery, but also about documenting and sharing the precious experiences of different cultures.
